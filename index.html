About Me

Hello! I'm Guillem Burgués, a Computer Engineer with a major on Information Technology by the Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya (UPC).

After five years of working, I did not feel fulfilled with the path my carrer had taken so I decided to make a change. This change led me to enroll at the Master's Degree in Advanced Programming for AAA Video Games at UPC.

Videogames have always been a hobby of mine, and now I have the knowledge and experience to start working on something people and myself enjoy.
